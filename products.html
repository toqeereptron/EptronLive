ID-ME

Id-me is a technology that allows to identify and confirm a person by measuring biological (anatomical and physiological) characteristics. Biometrics are the only way to prove real presence, since it is based on the unique traits like fingerprints and face, which can’t be lent, stolen, guessed or shared. Id-me is ideal for businesses who want to secure their premises or access to files, programs and accounts, or accelerate the access procedure.

Id-me allows to add a biometric authentication to your existing applications and cloud services quickly and easily, either via standard protocols such as SAML or OpenID, or by using the included software development kit (SDK).
